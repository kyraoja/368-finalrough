About Me

My name is Kyra Oja and I am a Visual Communication Design student at Eastern Washington University.I currently work at the EWU Extended Campus as a graphic designer, and earlier this year was a graphic design intern for the Spokane Shock Arena Football Team.

I work with digital and print mediums, creating logos and identity branding.

Services

My services include:

  • Logos and Identity Branding
  • Business Cards and Letterheads
  • Banners
  • Brochures, Flyers, and Ads
  • Invitations
